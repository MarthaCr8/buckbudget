🧠 Budget wisdom

Dave Ramsey
Every dollar needs a name. A zero-based budget means income minus outgo equals zero — you tell every dollar where to go instead of wondering where it went.
The 50/30/20 Rule
50% of take-home to needs, 30% to wants, 20% to savings and debt payoff. It's a starting framework — not a law. Your situation might call for 60/20/20 or tighter.
Suze Orman
An emergency fund isn't optional. It's the first thing that stands between you and debt. Before you spend on wants, make sure you have at least $1,000 set aside, then build to 3–6 months.

Who We Are

BuckBudget is a personal finance application created by Matt Neal ("we," "us," or "our"). BuckBudget helps individuals budget, plan for retirement, track savings goals, and manage bills — without requiring access to your bank accounts or any sensitive financial credentials.

What Data We Collect

Data Stored Locally On Your Device

When you use BuckBudget, the following is saved directly to your device and never transmitted to us:

  • Your first name (if entered during onboarding)
  • Budget figures you enter (income, expenses, debt amounts)
  • Retirement calculator inputs (age, savings, goals)
  • Savings goals, bill reminders, and credit card entries
  • App settings and preferences

Optional: Email Address

During onboarding you may optionally provide your email. If you do, it's used solely to send BuckBudget product updates via Mailchimp. We will never sell your email. You can unsubscribe at any time.

What We Do NOT Collect

  • Bank account information or credentials
  • Social Security number or tax ID
  • Location, contacts, camera, or microphone
  • Any financial data — it stays on your device
  • Advertising data — we don't sell your data
How Your Data is Stored

All financial data is stored using your device's local storage and IndexedDB — standard browser storage that stays on your device. This means:

  • Your data exists only on the device you use
  • Clearing app data or uninstalling will delete your data
  • We have no ability to recover deleted data
  • Nothing is backed up to any cloud server by BuckBudget
Push Notifications

BuckBudget may request permission to send push notifications for bill reminders. This is optional. Notifications are generated locally on your device based on bill dates you enter — no content is sent to our servers. You can revoke permission in your device settings at any time.

Third-Party Services

Mailchimp (Optional Email)

If you opt in with your email, it's sent to Mailchimp per their privacy policy at mailchimp.com/legal/privacy.

Google Fonts & Chart.js

BuckBudget loads fonts from Google Fonts and charts from cdnjs.cloudflare.com. These may log your IP as standard web traffic. No financial data is shared.

Netlify (Hosting)

BuckBudget is hosted on Netlify, which may collect standard server logs including IP addresses. No financial app data is accessible to Netlify.

Children's Privacy

BuckBudget is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us information, contact us so we can delete it.

Your Rights & Choices
  • Access: All your data is visible directly within the app

  • Email opt-out: Use the unsubscribe link in any email or contact us directly
  • Notifications: Revoke permission in your device settings at any time
