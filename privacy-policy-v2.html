Privacy Policy

📅 Effective Date: March 28, 2026  ·  Last Updated: March 28, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Matt Neal ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the BuckBudget app and website located at buckbudget17.netlify.app (the "App").

By using BuckBudget you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

1. Information We Collect

Information you provide directly:

  • Name — collected during onboarding to personalize your experience. Stored locally on your device only.
  • Email address — optionally collected during onboarding. If provided, your email is added to our mailing list to send you BuckBudget updates and news. You can unsubscribe at any time.

Financial data you enter:

  • Income, expenses, debt balances, savings goals, and bill reminders you enter into BuckBudget are stored locally on your device only using your browser's localStorage.
  • This financial data is never transmitted to our servers, never shared with third parties, and never stored in any cloud database.
  • We cannot see, access, or recover your financial data. It exists only on your device.

Information collected automatically:

  • We do not use analytics, tracking pixels, or advertising cookies.
  • We do not collect IP addresses, device identifiers, or browsing behavior.
  • Basic server logs may be maintained by our hosting provider (Netlify) for security purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Your name is used solely to personalize your in-app experience (e.g. "Welcome back, Matt!")
  • Your email address is used to send you occasional updates about BuckBudget — new features, tips, and announcements. You can unsubscribe at any time.
  • Your financial data is used only to power the app's calculators and displays on your device. It never leaves your device.

3. Email Marketing

If you provide your email address during onboarding, it is added to our mailing list managed by Mailchimp. You can unsubscribe at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link in any email we send, or by contacting us directly at [email protected].

Mailchimp's privacy policy can be found at mailchimp.com/legal/privacy.

4. Data Storage & Security

  • All financial data is stored locally on your device using browser localStorage. It is not encrypted at rest, so we recommend not using BuckBudget on shared or public devices.
  • Email addresses are stored securely on Mailchimp's servers.
  • We use HTTPS encryption for all data transmitted between your device and our servers.
  • We do not store passwords or payment information of any kind.

5. Third Party Services

BuckBudget uses the following third party services:

  • Netlify — website hosting. Privacy policy: netlify.com/privacy
  • Mailchimp — email list management. Privacy policy: mailchimp.com/legal/privacy
  • Google Fonts — typography. Privacy policy: policies.google.com/privacy
  • Chart.js — data visualization (no data collection)

6. Children's Privacy

BuckBudget is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it immediately.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Unsubscribe from our email list at any time
  • Clear your local financial data at any time by clearing your browser's localStorage

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at [email protected].

8. Changes To This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of BuckBudget after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
