Legal · Privacy
Your Privacy is
Our Foundation
BuckBudget was built with one rule: your financial data is yours and yours alone. We don't sell it, share it, or store it on any server. Here's exactly how it works.
🔒 The Short Version

BuckBudget stores all of your financial data locally on your device only. We never see your numbers, your name, or your bank information. No bank login is ever required. Your data never leaves your phone.

Who We Are

BuckBudget is a personal finance application created by Matt Neal ("we," "us," or "our"). BuckBudget is designed to help individuals budget, plan for retirement, track savings goals, and manage bills — all without requiring access to your bank accounts or any sensitive financial credentials.

If you have any questions about this policy, you can reach us at the contact information provided at the bottom of this page.

What Data We Collect

Data Stored Locally On Your Device

When you use BuckBudget, the following information is saved directly to your device using your browser's local storage. This data never leaves your device and is never transmitted to us:

  • Your first name (if you choose to enter it during onboarding)
  • Budget figures you enter (income, expenses, debt amounts)
  • Retirement calculator inputs (age, savings, goals)
  • Savings goals you create
  • Bill reminders you add
  • Credit card payoff calculator entries
  • App settings and preferences

Optional: Email Address

During onboarding, you have the option (not required) to provide your email address. If you choose to do so, your email is used solely to send you BuckBudget product updates and new feature announcements. We use a third-party email service (Mailchimp) to manage this list. You can unsubscribe at any time. We will never sell your email address or use it for any other purpose.

What We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your bank account information
  • We do not require any login or account creation
  • We do not collect your Social Security number or tax ID
  • We do not track your location
  • We do not use advertising trackers or sell your data to advertisers
  • We do not access your contacts, camera, or microphone
  • We do not share your financial data with any third party
How Your Data is Stored

All financial data you enter into BuckBudget is stored using your device's local storage — the same technology browsers use to remember your preferences on websites. This means:

  • Your data exists only on the device you use the app on
  • Clearing your browser cache or app data will delete your BuckBudget data
  • Uninstalling the app will delete your data
  • We have no ability to recover your data if it is deleted
  • Your data is not backed up to any cloud server by BuckBudget

We recommend periodically noting down your key figures in a safe place in case your device data is cleared.

Push Notifications

BuckBudget may request permission to send you push notifications for bill reminders. This is entirely optional. If you grant permission:

  • Notifications are generated locally on your device based on bill dates you enter
  • No notification content is sent to or processed by our servers
  • You can revoke notification permission at any time in your device settings
Third-Party Services

Mailchimp (Optional Email List)

If you opt in with your email during onboarding, your email is sent to Mailchimp, our email marketing provider. Mailchimp stores your email on their servers according to their own privacy policy. You can view Mailchimp's privacy policy at mailchimp.com/legal/privacy.

Google Fonts & Chart.js

BuckBudget loads fonts from Google Fonts and a charting library (Chart.js) from a content delivery network (cdnjs.cloudflare.com). These services may log your IP address as part of standard web traffic handling, subject to their own privacy policies. No financial data is shared with these services.

Netlify (Hosting)

BuckBudget is hosted on Netlify. Netlify may collect standard web server logs including IP addresses and browser information. This is standard hosting behavior and no financial data from the app is accessible to Netlify.

Children's Privacy

BuckBudget is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

Your Rights & Choices

Because BuckBudget stores data locally on your device, you have full control over your data at all times:

  • Access: All your data is visible directly within the app
  • Deletion: Clear your browser/app local storage to delete all data instantly
  • Email opt-out: Unsubscribe from any email we send using the unsubscribe link, or contact us directly
  • Notification opt-out: Revoke notification permission in your device settings at any time

If you provided your email and want it removed from our list, contact us at the address below and we will remove it within 7 business days.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make significant changes, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the bottom of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of BuckBudget after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
